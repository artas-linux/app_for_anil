Acceptable Use Policy

Effective Date: October 8, 2025

Services We Provide

At DevSolutions, we process payments only for our own IT services and products, including:

  • Software & Web Development: corporate websites, e-commerce builds, custom apps, API & payment integrations
  • SaaS / Licenses: hosted tools (ticketing, CRM lite, inventory), monthly/annual billing
  • Maintenance & Support Plans: Bronze/Silver/Gold with fixed PKR pricing and clear response times
  • Training & Consulting: short courses, corporate training, hourly consulting (scope-bound)
  • Hosting & Domains (Reseller): pricing and renewal terms clearly published
  • Optional Hardware/Peripherals (when actually supplied): showing models, warranties, and delivery terms

Prohibited Services

We DO NOT accept payments for the following categories:

  • Cryptocurrency transactions or services
  • Forex/CFD trading platforms
  • Binary options platforms
  • Remittances or wallet top-ups
  • Person-to-person transfers
  • Gambling or betting platforms
  • Adult content services
  • Firearms or weapons
  • Pharmaceuticals (without proper licensing)
  • Donations or charity collections
  • Any third-party settlements

Compliance Commitment

By using our services, you confirm that your project does not fall into any of the prohibited categories listed above. We reserve the right to decline projects that we determine to be high-risk or outside our acceptable use guidelines.

Review Process

All projects undergo a review process to ensure they align with our acceptable use policy. We may request additional documentation to verify the nature of your business before beginning work.

Reporting Violations

If you become aware of any use of our services that violates this policy, please contact us immediately at compliance@devsolutions.pk.

Policy Updates

This Acceptable Use Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in regulations, payment gateway requirements, or business needs. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Questions about this policy? Contact us at compliance@devsolutions.pk
